Image Optimizer

What it does

Image Optimizer helps you shrink images for the web. Drop your files, compare the output across modern formats — AVIF, WebP, and JPEG — and download the smallest result. Smaller images mean less data for visitors to download and faster page loads.

How it works

Drop images onto the page or pick files with the keyboard shortcut (Ctrl+U on Windows and Linux, ⌘U on Mac). The app compresses each image in your browser using native codecs and shows you a side-by-side preview with file sizes so you can choose the best format for each image.

Privacy

Processing happens in your browser. Image Optimizer does not upload your files to a server; your images stay on your device. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Supported formats

You can open common image formats (JPEG, PNG, WebP, AVIF). Export is available as AVIF, WebP, and JPEG — the formats best suited for web delivery.
