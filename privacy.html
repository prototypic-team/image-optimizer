Privacy Policy

Overview

Image Optimizer is a client-side application that runs entirely in your web browser. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

Image Optimizer does not collect, store, or analyze any user data. The app does not use cookies, local storage, session storage, or any other persistent storage. It does not send analytics, track usage, or communicate with any backend servers.

Where Your Data Stays

All processing happens locally in your browser. Images you open and any other data you work with remain on your device. Nothing is transmitted to or stored on external servers. When you close the app or refresh the page, in-memory data is cleared.

Third-Party Code

The app uses open-source libraries (such as jSquash for image codec processing) that run entirely in your browser. These libraries do not initiate network requests or transmit data to third parties.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The current version is always available on this page.

Contact

For privacy-related requests, contact i0@prototypic.org.
